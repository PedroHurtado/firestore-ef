Pipeline

    Usuario (LINQ)
        ↓
    DbContext.Set<T>().Where(...)
        ↓
    FirestoreQueryProvider
        ↓
    FirestoreQueryCompilationContext
        ↓
    FirestoreQueryableMethodTranslatingExpressionVisitor
        ↓
    FirestoreQueryExpression (AST)
        ↓
    FirestoreShapedQueryCompilingExpressionVisitor
        ↓
    FirestoreQueryingEnumerable / FirestoreAggregationQueryingEnumerable
    /FirestoreProjectionQueryingEnumerable 
        ↓
    FirestoreQueryExecutor
        ↓
    FirestoreClientWrapper
        ↓
    Google.Cloud.Firestore SDK
        ↓
    Firestore (red)

Traking

    Visitor (compila query)
        ↓
    Pasa isTracking al Executor
        ↓
    Executor.ExecuteQueryAsync<T>(..., isTracking)
        ↓
    Executor deserializa + carga includes + hace tracking
        ↓
    Retorna IAsyncEnumerable<T> (entidades ya trackeadas)
        ↓
    Visitor solo itera y retorna
